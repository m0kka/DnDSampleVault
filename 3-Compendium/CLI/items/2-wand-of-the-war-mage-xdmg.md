# +2 Wand of the War Mage
*Rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster)*  

- **Weight**: 1.0 lbs.

While holding this wand, you gain a +2 bonus to spell attack rolls. In addition, you ignore [Cover](3-Compendium/CLI/rules/variant-rules/cover-xphb.md) when making a spell attack roll.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 322. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
