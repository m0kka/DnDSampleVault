# Hat of Disguise
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](items/XDMG/Hat of Disguise.webp#right)  


While wearing this hat, you can cast the [Disguise Self](/3-Compendium/CLI/spells/disguise-self-xphb.md) spell. The spell ends if the hat is removed.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 266. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
