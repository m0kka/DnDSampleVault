# Signal Whistle
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 5 cp

When blown as a [Utilize](actions.md#Utilize) action, a Signal Whistle produces a sound that can be heard up to 600 feet away.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 228. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
