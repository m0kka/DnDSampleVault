# Bloodrage Greataxe
*Weapon (greataxe), uncommon*  

- **Damage**: 1d12 S
- **Weight**: 7.0 lbs.

You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic greataxe while you have half your hit points or fewer.

*Source: The Book of Many Things p. 66*
