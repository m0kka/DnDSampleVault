# Waterskin
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 2 sp
- **Weight**: 5.0 lbs.

A Waterskin holds up to 4 pints. If you don't drink sufficient water, you risk dehydration.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 229. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
