# Shovel
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 2 gp
- **Weight**: 5.0 lbs.

Working for 1 hour, you can use a Shovel to dig a hole that is 5 feet on each side in soil or similar material.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 228. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
