# The Devourer
*Source: Eberron: Rising from the Last War p. 141* 
![The Dark Six](deities/ERLW/The Dark Six.webp#symbol)

- **Alignment**: Unaligned
- **Category**: The Dark Six
- **Domains**: Tempest
- **Pantheon**: Eberron
- **Province**: Nature's wrath
