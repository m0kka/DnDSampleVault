# Animal Friendship
*1st-level, Enchantment*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, a morsel of food
- **Duration:** 24 hour

Target a Beast that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the [Charmed](conditions.md#Charmed) condition for the duration. If you or one of your allies deals damage to the target, the spells ends.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You can target one additional Beast for each spell slot level above 1.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 239. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
