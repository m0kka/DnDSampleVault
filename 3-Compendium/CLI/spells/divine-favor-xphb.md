# Divine Favor
*1st-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 bonus
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 minute

Until the spell ends, your attacks with weapons deal an extra `1d4` Radiant damage on a hit.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 265. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
